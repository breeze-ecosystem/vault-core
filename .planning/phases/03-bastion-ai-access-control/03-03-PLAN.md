## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-13 | Spoofing | Face enrollment — photo upload bypass | mitigate | All face endpoints gated by @RequiresModule("advanced_facial_recognition"). @Roles("ADMIN","SUPER_ADMIN","GLOBAL_ADMIN"). Photo validated by Zod (min length). |
| T-03-14 | Elevation of Privilege | GLOBAL_ADMIN accessing unauthorized child org data | mitigate | TenantIsolationGuard extended to verify parentOrganizationId match (D-03). Only declared child orgs accessible. Mixed data sharing per D-04: only shared data visible cross-site. |
