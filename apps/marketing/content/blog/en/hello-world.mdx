## A New Era for Physical Security

Physical security is at an inflection point. Organizations are moving away from fragmented, point-solution architectures toward unified platforms that correlate data across video, access control, and operational systems.

Oversight Hub is purpose-built for this new reality.

### What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional systems that require operators to switch between multiple dashboards — one for cameras, another for door access, a third for incident management — Oversight Hub brings everything together in a single, intelligent pane of glass.

```typescript
// Example: Correlate an access control event with video evidence
const event = await prisma.accessEvent.findUnique({
  where: { id: eventId },
  include: {
    camera: true,
    door: true,
    alert: true,
  },
});

// AI analysis runs in real-time against the video clip
const analysis = await aiService.analyzeFrame({
  cameraId: event.camera.id,
  timestamp: event.timestamp,
});
```

### AI-First by Design

Every event flows through our AI analysis pipeline. From tailgating detection to anomaly scoring, the platform learns your facility's normal patterns and surfaces only what matters.

### Self-Hosted & Private

Your video never leaves your infrastructure. Oversight Hub is designed from the ground up for on-premise deployment — no cloud dependency, no data residency concerns.
