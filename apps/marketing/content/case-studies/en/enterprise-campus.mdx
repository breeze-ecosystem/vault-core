## The Challenge

Industrial Tech Group managed 12 buildings across 3 industrial sites with disparate security systems. Each site used a different access control system, isolated surveillance cameras, and manual incident management processes. Security teams had to switch between 4 different dashboards to correlate a single event.

With over 5,000 employees and 150 active badges per site, access management was an administrative nightmare. The lack of centralized visibility made it impossible to detect trends or identify security gaps before it was too late.

## The Solution

Oversight Hub deployed a unified platform across all 12 buildings in 8 weeks. The integration was achieved without replacing existing infrastructure — existing door controllers and cameras were connected via Edge Agent gateways.

The deployment included:

- **Unified Access Control**: All badges, doors, and access zones centralized in a single dashboard, with dynamic rules applied in real time.
- **Intelligent Video Surveillance**: All 240 existing cameras were integrated with real-time AI analysis, enabling automatic tailgating detection and suspicious behavior alerts.
- **Contextual Alerts**: Every door alert or access event is automatically correlated with the corresponding video, giving operators complete context without manual searching.
- **Automated Reporting**: Compliance reports and security audits are generated automatically, reducing administrative workload by 80%.

## The Results

Within 6 months, the Group achieved transformative results:

- **40% reduction in security incidents** through proactive detection and real-time alerts.
- **60% savings on security operations** through automation of manual processes and centralized management.
- **70% faster response time** — operators went from 15 minutes to 3 minutes on average to handle an incident.
- **4-month ROI** through operational savings and reduced security-related losses.
