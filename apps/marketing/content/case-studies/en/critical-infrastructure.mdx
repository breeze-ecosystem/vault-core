## The Challenge

The National Energy Operator manages a network of 25 sensitive sites — power plants, transformer stations, and control centers — spread across the country. Each site is subject to strict regulatory requirements for physical security.

The operator faced several critical challenges: heterogeneous security systems with no interoperability, intrusion detection relying solely on physical perimeters, and manual compliance processes that occupied a dedicated full-time team.

## The Solution

Oversight Hub was progressively deployed across all 25 sites, with a multi-layered security architecture:

- **Continuous AI Monitoring**: Real-time video analysis across all sites with automatic intrusion detection, suspicious behavior alerts, and anomaly detection.
- **Unified Access Control**: Centralized access management for all sites with dynamic policies adapted to each sensitivity level.
- **Multi-Site Correlation**: A consolidated view of security events across the entire network, with contextual alerts enriched by AI.
- **Automated Compliance Reporting**: Automatic generation of reports required by regulatory authorities, with complete timestamped access history.

## The Results

The Oversight Hub deployment delivered significant improvements:

- **Real-time threat detection** across all 25 sites, with a false positive rate below 2%.
- **70% faster response time** through contextual alerts and automatic event correlation.
- **Automated regulatory compliance** — audit reports generated with a single click, eliminating weeks of manual work per quarter.
- **99.98% platform availability** in the first year of operation, exceeding contractual requirements.

"The security of our sites is non-negotiable. Oversight Hub gave us the visibility and control we needed, while automating the compliance processes that consumed valuable resources. It's a strategic investment that has already proven its value."
— Head of Security Operations, National Energy Operator
